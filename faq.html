Frequently Asked Questions

Why does brewing water matter?
Water makes up 90–95% of beer. Minerals in water affect mash efficiency, hop expression, and mouthfeel. The right profile makes your beer shine.
How do I send a water sample?
Collect ~10-16 oz of tap water in a clean plastic bottle. No need to refrigerate. Ship it to us using regular mail — simple and fast.
How long does testing take?
Turnaround is typically 3 business days after we receive your sample. We’ll email you your invoice followed by the results.
Do I need to treat every batch?
Yes — water chemistry shifts with each beer style. Our packets are tailored for mash and sparge water, so you get consistent results every brew day.
What if I don’t know which profile to choose?
No problem! Just pick your beer style in our form. We’ll match it with the best water profile.
Can I order packets without testing?
Yes. If you already know your water, using Reverse Osmosis (RO)/distilled water or just want to experiment, you can order style-based packets directly.
What’s inside a treatment packet?
Each packet contains precise amounts of brewing salts (like calcium chloride, gypsum, or Epsom) measured to hit the target profile without guesswork.

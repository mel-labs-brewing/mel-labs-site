Custom Water Treatment Packets

Take your brewing to the next level! Fill out form below. We will prepare your packets and send you an invoice. One paid, we will send you your packets in time for your next brew day! Alongside our water testing service, we now offer custom water treatment packets made from food-grade minerals:

  • Gypsum (Calcium Sulfate)
  • Calcium Chloride
  • Epsom Salt (Magnesium Sulfate)
  • Calcium Hydroxide (Slaked Lime)
  • Baking Soda (Sodium Bicarbonate)
  • Canning Salt (Sodium Chloride)

How It Works

  1. Choose your water type: either use RO water or send us a sample for testing.
  2. Tell us your batch details: mash water volume, sparge water volume, and beer style.
  3. We send you packets: one for mash water, one for sparge water, tailored to your brew.

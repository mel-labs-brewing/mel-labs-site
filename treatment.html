Custom Water Treatment Packets

Take your brewing to the next level! Simply fill out the form below, and we’ll prepare your packets and send you an invoice. Once payment is received, your custom water treatment packets will be on their way in time for your next brew day.

Our packets are made with food-grade brewing salts including Gypsum (Calcium Sulfate), Calcium Chloride, Epsom Salt (Magnesium Sulfate), Calcium Hydroxide (Slaked Lime), Baking Soda (Sodium Bicarbonate), and Canning Salt (Sodium Chloride).

The process is simple: you choose whether you’re brewing with RO water or sending us a sample for testing. Next, tell us your mash and sparge water volumes along with the beer style. We’ll create two packets—one for your mash water and one for your sparge water—tailored to your specific recipe.

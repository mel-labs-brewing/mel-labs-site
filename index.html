Affordable Water Testing for Homebrewers

Send us 12–16 oz of your water. We’ll test it, and you only pay when your results are ready. We will send an invoice by email. Available only within the USA right now.

All samples will be tested for chloride, sulfates, total alkalinity, bicarbonate, residual alkalinity, total hardness, calcium hardness, magnesium hardness, sodium, and pH. In addtion, we can save you money by building your mash and sparge treatment packets by navigating to the Treatment Packet tab.
